# Marketing and Brand Management - Phase 2 Processes Backlog

This document contains the prioritized backlog of processes to be implemented for the Marketing and Brand Management specialization. Each process represents a key workflow that marketing professionals execute regularly.

---

## Brand Strategy Processes

- [ ] **Brand Positioning Development** - Define brand's unique market position using target audience, frame of reference, points of differentiation, and reasons to believe. Create positioning statement and validate with target customers. [Reference](https://www.aprildunford.com/obviously-awesome)

- [ ] **Brand Architecture Design** - Structure the relationship between corporate brand, sub-brands, and product brands. Define naming conventions, visual hierarchy, and brand portfolio strategy. [Reference](https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/strategic-brand-management-building-measuring-and-managing-brand-equity/P200000005912/9780137852345)

- [ ] **Brand Guidelines Creation** - Develop comprehensive brand standards documentation including visual identity, voice and tone, messaging hierarchy, and usage rules across all touchpoints. [Reference](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/brand-guidelines/)

- [ ] **Brand Health Assessment** - Measure brand equity using Keller's CBBE model (salience, performance, imagery, judgments, feelings, resonance) through surveys and tracking studies. [Reference](https://journals.sagepub.com/home/jmx)

---

## Campaign Management Processes

- [ ] **Integrated Campaign Planning** - Develop cross-channel campaign strategy including objectives, target audience, messaging, channel mix (PESO model), budget allocation, timeline, and success metrics. [Reference](https://spinsucks.com/communication/peso-model/)

- [ ] **Campaign Creative Development** - Brief creative teams, develop concepts, produce assets across formats, ensure brand consistency, and manage approval workflows. [Reference](https://www.ama.org)

- [ ] **A/B Testing Program** - Design and execute marketing experiments for ads, landing pages, emails, and CTAs using statistical significance to optimize conversion rates. [Reference](https://cxl.com/blog/ab-testing/)

- [ ] **Campaign Performance Analysis** - Track campaign metrics against KPIs, analyze results by channel and audience segment, document learnings, and create optimization recommendations. [Reference](https://www.hubspot.com/marketing-funnel)

---

## Content Marketing Processes

- [ ] **Content Strategy Development** - Define content pillars, themes, and editorial mission aligned with business objectives and audience needs. Create content matrix mapping content types to funnel stages. [Reference](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/content-inc-book/)

- [ ] **Editorial Calendar Management** - Plan, schedule, and coordinate content production across formats (blog, video, podcast, social) with topic clusters and SEO integration. [Reference](https://annhandley.com/everybodywrites/)

- [ ] **Content Production Workflow** - Establish end-to-end content creation process from briefing through writing, editing, SEO optimization, approval, and publication. [Reference](https://www.marketingprofs.com)

- [ ] **Content Performance Optimization** - Analyze content engagement metrics, refresh underperforming content, update evergreen pieces, and implement content scoring for continuous improvement. [Reference](https://www.impactplus.com/they-ask-you-answer-book)

---

## Digital Marketing Processes

- [ ] **SEO Strategy Implementation** - Conduct keyword research, optimize on-page elements, build technical SEO foundation, develop link building strategy, and track organic search performance. [Reference](https://ahrefs.com)

- [ ] **SEM Campaign Management** - Set up and optimize paid search campaigns in Google Ads including keyword targeting, ad copy, bidding strategies, and quality score improvement. [Reference](https://skillshop.withgoogle.com/googleads)

- [ ] **Social Media Strategy Execution** - Define platform priorities, content mix, posting cadence, community management protocols, and engagement programs aligned with brand voice. [Reference](https://sproutsocial.com)

- [ ] **Email Marketing Automation** - Build email nurture sequences, lifecycle campaigns, triggered communications, and personalization rules using marketing automation platforms. [Reference](https://academy.hubspot.com)

- [ ] **Paid Social Advertising** - Plan and execute advertising campaigns on Meta, LinkedIn, and other social platforms with audience targeting, creative optimization, and conversion tracking. [Reference](https://www.facebookblueprint.com)

---

## Market Research Processes

- [ ] **Customer Segmentation Analysis** - Divide market into distinct groups using demographics, psychographics, behavior, and needs. Evaluate segment attractiveness and prioritize targets using STP framework. [Reference](https://www.marketing91.com/stp-marketing/)

- [ ] **Competitive Analysis Research** - Monitor competitor positioning, messaging, campaigns, and market share. Create competitive battlecards and identify differentiation opportunities. [Reference](https://www.semrush.com)

- [ ] **Customer Persona Development** - Create detailed buyer personas through qualitative research including goals, pain points, jobs-to-be-done, and content preferences. [Reference](https://www.hubspot.com/customer-journey-map)

- [ ] **Voice of Customer Program** - Systematically collect, analyze, and act on customer feedback through surveys, interviews, reviews, and social listening. [Reference](https://www.brandwatch.com)

---

## Marketing Analytics Processes

- [ ] **Attribution Modeling Setup** - Implement multi-touch attribution to understand channel contribution to conversions. Compare first-touch, last-touch, and data-driven models. [Reference](https://support.google.com/analytics/)

- [ ] **Marketing ROI Analysis** - Calculate return on investment by channel and campaign. Build marketing mix models to optimize budget allocation across the portfolio. [Reference](https://www.gartner.com/en/marketing)

- [ ] **Marketing Dashboard Development** - Create executive dashboards tracking key metrics (CAC, LTV, conversion rates, brand awareness) with data visualization for stakeholder reporting. [Reference](https://www.tableau.com)

- [ ] **Customer Journey Analytics** - Map and measure customer touchpoints across channels, identify drop-off points, and optimize conversion funnels through data analysis. [Reference](https://www.forrester.com/books/outside-in/)
