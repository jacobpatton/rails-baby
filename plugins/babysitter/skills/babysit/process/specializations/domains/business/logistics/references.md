## Books

### Foundational Texts

**"Supply Chain Management: Strategy, Planning, and Operation" by Sunil Chopra and Peter Meindl**
- Leading textbook on supply chain management including logistics
- Comprehensive coverage of transportation, inventory, and network design
- Quantitative methods for logistics optimization
- URL: https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/supply-chain-management-strategy-planning-and-operation/P200000003281

**"The Handbook of Logistics and Distribution Management" by Alan Rushton, Phil Croucher, and Peter Baker**
- Comprehensive reference covering all aspects of logistics
- Practical guidance for logistics operations and strategy
- Updated editions cover emerging trends and technologies
- URL: https://www.koganpage.com/logistics-operations-management/the-handbook-of-logistics-and-distribution-management-9781398600744

**"Logistics and Supply Chain Management" by Martin Christopher**
- Foundational text on logistics and supply chain integration
- Strategic approach to logistics excellence
- Focus on creating customer value through logistics
- URL: https://www.pearson.com/en-gb/subject-catalog/p/logistics-and-supply-chain-management/P200000005009

**"Contemporary Logistics" by Paul R. Murphy Jr. and A. Michael Knemeyer**
- Comprehensive introduction to logistics management
- Coverage of domestic and international logistics
- Emphasis on contemporary issues and trends
- URL: https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/contemporary-logistics/P200000003277

### Transportation Management

**"Transportation: A Global Supply Chain Perspective" by Robert A. Novack, Brian Gibson, Yoshinori Suzuki, and John J. Coyle**
- Comprehensive transportation management textbook
- Coverage of all transportation modes
- Regulatory environment and carrier management
- URL: https://www.cengage.com/c/transportation-a-global-supply-chain-perspective-9e-novack/

**"Freight Transport and Distribution" by David Lowe**
- Practical guide to freight operations
- Road haulage, intermodal, and distribution management
- UK and European perspective with global applicability
- URL: https://www.koganpage.com/logistics-operations-management/lowes-transport-managers-and-operators-handbook-2024-9781398615090

**"The Definitive Guide to Transportation" by CSCMP**
- Comprehensive resource from Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals
- Best practices in transportation management
- Technology and analytics for transportation
- URL: https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/definitive-guide-to-transportation-the-principles-strategies-and-decisions-for-the-effective-flow-of-goods-and-services/P200000003285

## Standards and Guidelines

### Transportation Standards

**International Air Transport Association (IATA)**
- Air cargo regulations and standards
- Dangerous goods regulations
- Unit Load Device (ULD) standards
- URL: https://www.iata.org/en/publications/

**International Maritime Organization (IMO)**
- Maritime shipping regulations
- IMDG Code for dangerous goods
- SOLAS safety standards
- URL: https://www.imo.org/

**Department of Transportation (DOT) / FMCSA**
- Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations
- Hours of Service rules
- Hazardous materials transportation
- URL: https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/regulations

**49 CFR - Transportation of Hazardous Materials**
- US regulations for hazmat shipping
- Classification, packaging, and labeling requirements
- Modal requirements for all transportation modes
- URL: https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-49

**Incoterms 2020 (ICC)**
- International commercial terms
- Defines responsibilities for shipping and delivery
- Standard for global trade
- URL: https://iccwbo.org/resources-for-business/incoterms-rules/incoterms-2020/

### Warehouse and Storage Standards

**OSHA Warehouse Safety Guidelines**
- Occupational safety requirements for warehouses
- Powered industrial truck standards
- Ergonomics and material handling safety
- URL: https://www.osha.gov/warehousing

**NFPA Standards for Storage**
- Fire protection for warehouses
- Sprinkler and detection requirements
- Storage arrangement and commodity classification
- URL: https://www.nfpa.org/

**Rack Manufacturers Institute (RMI)**
- Storage rack design standards
- Installation and safety guidelines
- Load capacity and inspection requirements
- URL: https://www.mhi.org/rmi

### Food and Pharmaceutical Logistics

**FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)**
- Sanitary transportation of food
- Temperature control requirements
- Traceability and record keeping
- URL: https://www.fda.gov/food/guidance-regulation-food-and-dietary-supplements/food-safety-modernization-act-fsma

**Good Distribution Practice (GDP)**
- Pharmaceutical distribution standards
- Temperature-controlled logistics
- Quality management for distribution
- URL: https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789241506014

**COOL - Country of Origin Labeling**
- Labeling requirements for imported goods
- Compliance for food products
- Documentation requirements
- URL: https://www.ams.usda.gov/rules-regulations/cool

### Supply Chain Standards

**GS1 Standards**
- Global standards for barcoding and identification
- GTIN, SSCC, and GLN identifiers
- EDI and data synchronization
- URL: https://www.gs1.org/

**APICS SCOR Model**
- Supply Chain Operations Reference model
- Process framework including logistics
- Metrics and best practices
- URL: https://www.ascm.org/corporate-transformation/standards/scor/

**ISO 28000 - Supply Chain Security**
- Security management for supply chain
- Risk assessment and management
- Certification standard
- URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/79612.html

**ISO 9001 - Quality Management**
- Quality management system standard
- Process approach to quality
- Applicable to logistics operations
- URL: https://www.iso.org/iso-9001-quality-management.html

**C-TPAT (Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism)**
- US Customs security partnership
- Supply chain security guidelines
- Importer compliance requirements
- URL: https://www.cbp.gov/border-security/ports-entry/cargo-security/ctpat
