# Business Strategy and Operations - References

A curated collection of authoritative resources for Business Strategy and Operations professionals.

---

## Books

### Strategy Fundamentals

1. **Good to Great: Why Some Companies Make the Leap and Others Don't**
   - Author: Jim Collins
   - Publisher: HarperBusiness
   - URL: https://www.jimcollins.com/books.html
   - Description: Landmark study of companies that made the transition from good to great, identifying key factors for sustained excellence.

2. **Blue Ocean Strategy: How to Create Uncontested Market Space and Make the Competition Irrelevant**
   - Authors: W. Chan Kim and Renee Mauborgne
   - Publisher: Harvard Business Review Press
   - URL: https://www.blueoceanstrategy.com/books/
   - Description: Framework for creating new market spaces through value innovation rather than competing in existing markets.

3. **Competitive Strategy: Techniques for Analyzing Industries and Competitors**
   - Author: Michael E. Porter
   - Publisher: Free Press
   - URL: https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/item.aspx?num=195
   - Description: Foundational text on competitive analysis introducing Five Forces framework and generic strategies.

4. **Competitive Advantage: Creating and Sustaining Superior Performance**
   - Author: Michael E. Porter
   - Publisher: Free Press
   - URL: https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/item.aspx?num=193
   - Description: Introduces value chain analysis and explains how firms can achieve and sustain competitive advantage.

5. **The Strategy-Focused Organization: How Balanced Scorecard Companies Thrive in the New Business Environment**
   - Authors: Robert S. Kaplan and David P. Norton
   - Publisher: Harvard Business Review Press
   - URL: https://hbr.org/product/the-strategy-focused-organization-how-balanced-scorecard-companies-thrive-in-the-new-business-environment/8377-HBK-ENG
   - Description: Comprehensive guide to implementing the Balanced Scorecard for strategic management.

### Business Operations and Excellence

6. **The Goal: A Process of Ongoing Improvement**
   - Author: Eliyahu M. Goldratt
   - Publisher: North River Press
   - URL: https://www.toc-goldratt.com/en/product/The-Goal-A-Process-of-Ongoing-Improvement
   - Description: Novel introducing Theory of Constraints and systematic approach to operational improvement.

7. **The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses**
   - Author: Eric Ries
   - Publisher: Currency
   - URL: https://theleanstartup.com/book
   - Description: Methodology for developing businesses and products through validated learning and rapid iteration.

8. **The Toyota Way: 14 Management Principles from the World's Greatest Manufacturer**
   - Author: Jeffrey K. Liker
   - Publisher: McGraw-Hill
   - URL: https://www.mheducation.com/highered/product/toyota-way-second-edition-14-management-principles-from-world-s-greatest-manufacturer-liker/9780071392310.html
   - Description: Comprehensive examination of Toyota's management philosophy and lean principles.

9. **Measure What Matters: How Google, Bono, and the Gates Foundation Rock the World with OKRs**
   - Author: John Doerr
   - Publisher: Portfolio
   - URL: https://www.whatmatters.com/the-book
   - Description: Practical guide to implementing Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) for goal setting and execution.

10. **The Innovator's Dilemma: When New Technologies Cause Great Firms to Fail**
    - Author: Clayton M. Christensen
    - Publisher: Harvard Business Review Press
    - URL: https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/item.aspx?num=46
    - Description: Explains why leading companies fail to stay ahead of industry changes and how to avoid disruption.

### Strategic Planning and Execution

11. **Playing to Win: How Strategy Really Works**
    - Authors: A.G. Lafley and Roger L. Martin
    - Publisher: Harvard Business Review Press
    - URL: https://hbr.org/product/playing-to-win-how-strategy-really-works/12601-HBK-ENG
    - Description: Strategic framework based on five essential choices that guide winning strategies.

12. **Execution: The Discipline of Getting Things Done**
    - Authors: Larry Bossidy and Ram Charan
    - Publisher: Crown Business
    - URL: https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/58564/execution-by-larry-bossidy-and-ram-charan/
    - Description: Practical guide to turning strategies into results through disciplined execution.

13. **The Art of Strategy: A Game Theorist's Guide to Success in Business and Life**
    - Authors: Avinash K. Dixit and Barry J. Nalebuff
    - Publisher: W. W. Norton & Company
    - URL: https://wwnorton.com/books/The-Art-of-Strategy/
    - Description: Applies game theory to business strategy and decision-making.

14. **Built to Last: Successful Habits of Visionary Companies**
    - Authors: Jim Collins and Jerry I. Porras
    - Publisher: HarperBusiness
    - URL: https://www.jimcollins.com/books.html
    - Description: Research-based analysis of what makes enduring, visionary companies successful.

15. **Only the Paranoid Survive: How to Exploit the Crisis Points That Challenge Every Company**
    - Author: Andrew S. Grove
    - Publisher: Currency
    - URL: https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/72328/only-the-paranoid-survive-by-andrew-s-grove/
    - Description: Insights on recognizing and adapting to strategic inflection points.

### Business Transformation and Change

16. **Leading Change**
    - Author: John P. Kotter
    - Publisher: Harvard Business Review Press
    - URL: https://www.kotterinc.com/book/leading-change/
    - Description: Eight-step process for leading organizational change and transformation.

17. **Switch: How to Change Things When Change Is Hard**
    - Authors: Chip Heath and Dan Heath
    - Publisher: Currency
    - URL: https://heathbrothers.com/books/switch/
    - Description: Framework for making change easier by understanding the emotional and rational aspects of behavior.

18. **Reframing Organizations: Artistry, Choice, and Leadership**
    - Authors: Lee G. Bolman and Terrence E. Deal
    - Publisher: Jossey-Bass
    - URL: https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Reframing+Organizations%3A+Artistry%2C+Choice%2C+and+Leadership%2C+7th+Edition-p-9781119756842
    - Description: Four-frame model for understanding and managing organizations effectively.

## Papers and Research

### Foundational Strategy Papers

19. **What Is Strategy?**
    - Author: Michael E. Porter
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (1996)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/1996/11/what-is-strategy
    - Description: Definitive article distinguishing operational effectiveness from strategic positioning.

20. **The Core Competence of the Corporation**
    - Authors: C.K. Prahalad and Gary Hamel
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (1990)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/1990/05/the-core-competence-of-the-corporation
    - Description: Introduces the concept of core competencies as the root of competitive advantage.

21. **Profit Pools: A Fresh Look at Strategy**
    - Authors: Orit Gadiesh and James L. Gilbert
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (1998)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/1998/05/profit-pools-a-fresh-look-at-strategy
    - Description: Framework for understanding where profits are concentrated in an industry.

22. **The Five Competitive Forces That Shape Strategy**
    - Author: Michael E. Porter
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (2008)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/2008/01/the-five-competitive-forces-that-shape-strategy
    - Description: Updated and expanded treatment of the Five Forces framework.

### Business Model and Innovation

23. **Reinventing Your Business Model**
    - Authors: Mark W. Johnson, Clayton M. Christensen, and Henning Kagermann
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (2008)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/2008/12/reinventing-your-business-model
    - Description: Framework for business model innovation and transformation.

24. **Blue Ocean Leadership**
    - Authors: W. Chan Kim and Renee Mauborgne
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (2014)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/2014/05/blue-ocean-leadership
    - Description: Applies Blue Ocean Strategy principles to leadership and organizational change.

### Operations and Excellence

25. **Decoding the DNA of the Toyota Production System**
    - Authors: Steven Spear and H. Kent Bowen
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (1999)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/1999/09/decoding-the-dna-of-the-toyota-production-system
    - Description: Analysis of the underlying principles behind Toyota's operational excellence.

26. **The Balanced Scorecard - Measures That Drive Performance**
    - Authors: Robert S. Kaplan and David P. Norton
    - Publication: Harvard Business Review (1992)
    - URL: https://hbr.org/1992/01/the-balanced-scorecard-measures-that-drive-performance-2
    - Description: Original article introducing the Balanced Scorecard framework.
