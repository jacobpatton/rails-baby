# Humanities and Anthropology - References

## Foundational Academic Works

### Anthropology Classics

1. **"The Interpretation of Cultures" by Clifford Geertz**
   - https://www.basicbooks.com/titles/clifford-geertz/the-interpretation-of-cultures/9780465093557/
   - Foundational text on interpretive anthropology and thick description

2. **"Argonauts of the Western Pacific" by Bronislaw Malinowski**
   - https://www.routledge.com/Argonauts-of-the-Western-Pacific/Malinowski/p/book/9781138598041
   - Classic ethnography establishing participant observation methods

3. **"Coming of Age in Samoa" by Margaret Mead**
   - https://www.harpercollins.com/products/coming-of-age-in-samoa-margaret-mead
   - Influential study of adolescence and culture

4. **"Structural Anthropology" by Claude Levi-Strauss**
   - https://www.basicbooks.com/titles/claude-levi-strauss/structural-anthropology/9780465095162/
   - Foundation of structuralist anthropology

5. **"Writing Culture" edited by James Clifford and George Marcus**
   - https://www.ucpress.edu/books/writing-culture/paper
   - Critical examination of ethnographic writing

6. **"Europe and the People Without History" by Eric Wolf**
   - https://www.ucpress.edu/books/europe-and-the-people-without-history/paper
   - World-systems approach to anthropology

### Historical Theory and Method

7. **"The Historian's Craft" by Marc Bloch**
   - https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/297024/the-historians-craft-by-marc-bloch/
   - Classic meditation on historical method

8. **"Metahistory" by Hayden White**
   - https://press.jhu.edu/books/title/1702/metahistory
   - Influential analysis of historical narrative and rhetoric

9. **"The Great Cat Massacre" by Robert Darnton**
   - https://www.basicbooks.com/titles/robert-darnton/the-great-cat-massacre/9780465015566/
   - Exemplary cultural history and mentalites approach

10. **"The Return of Martin Guerre" by Natalie Zemon Davis**
    - https://www.hup.harvard.edu/books/9780674766914
    - Model of microhistory methodology

11. **"Provincializing Europe" by Dipesh Chakrabarty**
    - https://press.princeton.edu/books/paperback/9780691130019/provincializing-europe
    - Postcolonial critique of Eurocentric historiography

12. **"Silencing the Past" by Michel-Rolph Trouillot**
    - https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/246609/silencing-the-past-20th-anniversary-edition-by-michel-rolph-trouillot/
    - Critical analysis of power and historical production

### Literary Theory

13. **"S/Z" by Roland Barthes**
    - https://us.macmillan.com/books/9780374521677/sz
    - Influential structuralist/post-structuralist textual analysis

14. **"Of Grammatology" by Jacques Derrida**
    - https://press.jhu.edu/books/title/9886/grammatology
    - Foundational text of deconstruction

15. **"Orientalism" by Edward Said**
    - https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/77844/orientalism-by-edward-w-said/
    - Groundbreaking postcolonial literary and cultural criticism

16. **"Gender Trouble" by Judith Butler**
    - https://www.routledge.com/Gender-Trouble-Feminism-and-the-Subversion-of-Identity/Butler/p/book/9780415389556
    - Key text in gender and queer theory

17. **"The Location of Culture" by Homi K. Bhabha**
    - https://www.routledge.com/The-Location-of-Culture/Bhabha/p/book/9780415336390
    - Important postcolonial literary theory

18. **"Narrative Discourse" by Gerard Genette**
    - https://www.cornellpress.cornell.edu/book/9780801492594/narrative-discourse/
    - Foundational narratology text

### Linguistics Foundations

19. **"Course in General Linguistics" by Ferdinand de Saussure**
    - https://www.bloomsbury.com/us/course-in-general-linguistics-9781472512055/
    - Foundational text of modern linguistics

20. **"Syntactic Structures" by Noam Chomsky**
    - https://www.degruyter.com/document/doi/10.1515/9783110218329/html
    - Revolutionary work in generative grammar

21. **"Women, Fire, and Dangerous Things" by George Lakoff**
    - https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/book/chicago/W/bo3645099.html
    - Cognitive linguistics and categorization

22. **"Language and Social Identity" edited by John J. Gumperz**
    - https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/language-and-social-identity/E1A13FE3F07C4B28B3DBDE3E6BE4D5D4
    - Key sociolinguistics collection

23. **"Linguistic Typology and Language Universals" edited by Martin Haspelmath**
    - https://www.degruyter.com/document/doi/10.1515/9783110194265/html
    - Comprehensive linguistic typology handbook

24. **"The Cambridge Handbook of Endangered Languages" edited by Peter Austin and Julia Sallabank**
