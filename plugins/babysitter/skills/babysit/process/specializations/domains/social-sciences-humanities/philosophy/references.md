A curated collection of authoritative resources for Philosophy, Theology, Ethics, Logic, Metaphysics, Epistemology, Religious Studies, and Critical Thinking.

## Encyclopedias and Reference Works

### Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP)
The preeminent online philosophical encyclopedia, featuring peer-reviewed articles by experts.
- URL: https://plato.stanford.edu/
- Topics: All areas of philosophy
- Access: Free, open access

### Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy (IEP)
Peer-reviewed academic resource covering major philosophers, concepts, and movements.
- URL: https://iep.utm.edu/
- Topics: All areas of philosophy
- Access: Free, open access

### Routledge Encyclopedia of Philosophy
Comprehensive philosophical reference with over 2,800 articles.
- URL: https://www.rep.routledge.com/
- Topics: All areas of philosophy
- Access: Subscription required

### Encyclopedia of Religion (Macmillan)
Major reference work covering world religions comprehensively.
- URL: https://www.gale.com/databases/macmillan-reference-usa
- Topics: World religions, religious studies
- Access: Library subscription

### Catholic Encyclopedia (New Advent)
Encyclopedic resource on Catholic theology, history, and philosophy.
- URL: https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/
- Topics: Catholic theology, philosophy, history
- Access: Free, open access

### Jewish Encyclopedia
Historical encyclopedia of Jewish religion, history, and culture.
- URL: https://www.jewishencyclopedia.com/
