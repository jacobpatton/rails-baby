# Competitive Intelligence Skill

Deep competitive analysis and market monitoring capabilities for product strategy decisions.

## Purpose

This skill provides comprehensive competitive intelligence gathering and analysis to support product management decisions. It enables systematic tracking of competitors, market positioning analysis, and identification of strategic opportunities.

## Key Capabilities

- **Competitor Analysis**: Analyze competitor websites, pricing models, and market positioning
- **Feature Tracking**: Monitor competitor feature releases and compare capabilities
- **Market Monitoring**: Track industry trends, analyst reports, and market dynamics
- **Gap Identification**: Identify white space opportunities and market gaps
- **Battlecard Generation**: Create competitive battlecards for sales enablement

## Process Integration

Integrates with:
- Competitive Analysis Process
- Product Vision and Strategy Process
- Product Launch GTM Process
- Quarterly Roadmap Process

## Usage

Invoke this skill when conducting competitive research, preparing for launches, or updating strategic plans with market intelligence.
